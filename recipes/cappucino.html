Cappuccino

Let's Make a Frothy Fall Cappuccino!

Ingredients

  • Ground espresso blend
  • Milk
  • Flavored syrup of your choosing. (We chose hazelnut)
  • Cinnamon (optional)
  • You should also have an espresso machine and frother for this recipe!

Steps

  1. Using your espresso machine, prepare 2-3oz espresso.
  2. Next, prepare your frothed milk. (This should be a 1:1 ratio to your espresso)
  3. Pour your espresso into a mug, followed by the flavored syrup of your choosing.
  4. Pour the steamed milk and froth into your mug.
  5. Lastly, garnish with cinnamon or spice to add some additional flavor!
